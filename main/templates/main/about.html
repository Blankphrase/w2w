"W2W" - What To Watch

What To Watch (W2W) is a web-based application providing movies recommendation on the base of the users preferences. It's just the first installment of the app so we ask for a little bit of understanding. If you have any questions, remarks or you found a bug, please give us notice by e-mail.

The recommendations are made on the base of item-based collaborative filtering. To put is simple, when we prepare a recommendation for a user, we take into consideration the preferences of other users. Collaborative filtering algorithms are widly used in many domains and provide significant improvement in comparison with other methods. For the present moment, algorithm that is used by W2W is SlopeOne. Despite its simplicity, it is comparable to more complicated and computationally expensive algorithms. You can find more information about this algorithm on Wikipedia.

Functionalities

W2W provieds movies recommendation. In order to receive a recommendation a user has to explicitly state her/his preferences by grading selected movies on five points scale (1-5). There are two modes:

  • Stand-Alone Search
  • General Search

Stand-Alone Search was primarily designed for new and not authenticated users. However logged in users can also take adventage of this mode. You have to state your preferences from the very beginning every time you want a recommendation. It can be a little bit burdensome when one frequently makes use of W2W. That is why General Search mode was introduced.

General Search mode is intended only for signed up users. We remember your preferences, which you can adjust any time, and we prepare comprehensive recommendation on the base of all your preferences. You can create list of movies you want to watch - watchlist. You can also visits your past recommendations. Of-course if you would like receive new recommendation not related to your overall preferences, you can always choose Stand-Alone Search.

We would like to fully appreciate TMDB for providing data used by W2W. Thank You Very Much.

So explore a little. Search for your favorite movies. Build a list of movies you want to watch. We're really proud of the service we've built and hope you find it as useful.
